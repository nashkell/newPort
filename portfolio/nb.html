Building a consistent experience.

Environmental Graphics, Wayfinding Design

Neuberger Berman, one of the world’s leading employee-controlled investment managers, relocated its global headquarters in New York City in 2016 to a premier building in midtown Manhattan at 1290 Avenue of the Americas. Occupying approximately 355,000 square feet in its new home, Neuberger Berman required extensive branding and wayfinding elements throughout.

While at Poulin + Morris we created an environmental graphics and wayfinding sign program for Neuberger Berman's new home, that refers to the companies tenured and stable long standing investment culture of fundamental research and independent thinking.
