About me

Hello!


I'm a senior digital designer and front end developer based in NJ, building effective and meaningful experiences through user-centered design and dedicated research making connections informationally, thematically, and aesthetically. Enhancing avenues of communication through a focus on typography, web design, UX/UI design, interactive design, and motion graphics.

My body of work currently includes pieces ranging from website/application design to print communications and has been recognized for design excellence by Graphis. I am a member of The American Institute of Graphic Arts (AIGA) and the Society for Experiential Graphic Design (SEGD).

I am confident in Adobe Creative Suite, print and digital design. I have experience with HTML, CSS, jQuery and Javascript, AngularJS, GIT, SASS, Sketch.
